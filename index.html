About Fika

Fika is an informal, intentional break from the day's work that always involves coffee and the occasional pastry. Fika comes from the Swedish word, "kaffi" which means coffee. A fika can last anywhere from 15 minutes to an hour, or however long you desire!

Fika is all about appreciating the small moments of life. Sitting quietly with a good book and a latte, or chatting with friends over some macchiatos, either way is a good way to step back and enjoy life.

How to Fika

When one fikas, one must always have some sort of beverage, whether it be coffee or tea. Traditional pastries or sweet treats can accompany the fika. One may fika by oneself or take it with friends, depending on the time or place.
